HIS ONLY SON

Msc Dissertation Project

The screenplay is a structure that wants to become another structure. That structure has been and will continue to be the completed film. My disseration by practice project explored the potential of utilizing the affordances of recent web innovations, and the lessons to be drawn from recent trends in online long-form journalism, and other digital mixed media narratives, to create an intermediary art-form that helps to enrich the text, bring the screenplay alive and give it a broader dimension, that takes it a step closer to its completion on screen. The screenplay is the ideal structure for such a digital evolution.

Result: 1st Class Honours

Nothing is original. Steal from anywhere that resonates with inspiration or fuels your imagination. Devour old films, new films, music , books , paintings, photographs, poems, dreams, random conversations, architecture, bridges, street signs, trees, clouds, bodies of water, light and shadows. Select only things to steal from that speak directly to your soul. If you do this, your work (and theft) will be authentic. Authenticity is invaluable; originality is non-existent. And dont bother concealing your thievery - celebrate it if you feel like it. In any case, always remember what Jean-Luc Godard said - Its not where you take things from - its where you take them to.
